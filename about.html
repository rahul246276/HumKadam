Our Story

Born from a Belief that Love Deserves the Best

HumKadam was founded in 2008 by Mr. Rajesh Mehra and Mrs. Sunita Mehra with a simple yet powerful belief: every person deserves a life partner who understands them deeply, respects their values, and walks alongside them with love and commitment.

What started as a small consultancy in Connaught Place, Delhi, has grown into the capital's most respected matrimonial brand — with a team of 25+ expert consultants, a database of 12,000+ verified profiles, and over 3,800 successful marriages to our name.

We are not a website or an algorithm. We are real people, with real empathy, helping real families make the most important decision of their lives.
