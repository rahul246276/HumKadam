Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about HumKadam's services and process

How long does the matchmaking process take?

The timeline varies based on your preferences and requirements. On average, our clients find suitable matches within 2-4 months. Premium and Elite members often see faster results due to dedicated consultant support.

Are all profiles verified?

Yes, absolutely! Every profile undergoes rigorous verification including government ID verification, educational certificate checks, professional background screening, and family reference validation.

What is your success rate?

We're proud of our 98% client satisfaction rate and over 3,800 successful marriages since 2008. Our success comes from personalized attention and deep understanding of client needs.

Do you provide services for NRI clients?

Yes, we have a specialized NRI matchmaking service. Our team manages cross-border matches, handles time zone differences, and provides virtual consultation facilities for overseas clients.

How is my privacy protected?

Your privacy is our top priority. We never share your information without explicit consent. All profiles are password-protected, and we maintain strict confidentiality throughout the process.

Can families be involved in the process?

Absolutely! We understand Indian family values and encourage family involvement. Our family consultation services help ensure both families are comfortable and aligned with the match.
